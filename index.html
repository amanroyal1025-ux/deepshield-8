How DeepShield Works

6-stage AI pipeline for deepfake video analysis

STEP 01
🎞️

Frame Extraction

Video is split into equally-spaced frames using OpenCV for full temporal coverage.

STEP 02
👁️

Face Detection

Haar Cascade classifiers isolate and crop the face region from each frame.

STEP 03
🧠

Neural Network

EfficientNetB0 trained on deepfake datasets classifies each face as real or fake.

STEP 04
🔬

Artifact Analysis

Heuristic checks for edge noise, color inconsistency, and facial boundary blur.

STEP 05
📊

Score Aggregation

All frame predictions are averaged into a single robust confidence score.

STEP 06
🚨

Verdict & Alert

A FAKE or REAL verdict is returned with confidence level and recommendations.
